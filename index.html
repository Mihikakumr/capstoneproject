Food Wastage

It is becoming a trend of wasting food...Farmers try and work
their hardest to give us the best meal but we just throw
food and waste it! Poor people and beggars do not have money
to buy food but we have money and we waste food! It
is a sad thing to see food getting wasted. We shall always
try our best to save food.
